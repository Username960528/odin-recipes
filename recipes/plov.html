How to cook best plov according to ChatGpt

  • 2 cups of long-grain rice
  • 1 pound of lamb or beef, cut into small cubes
  • 2 large onions, finely chopped
  • 2-3 carrots, grated
  • 2 cups of water or beef broth
  • Salt and pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup of vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. In a large pot or Dutch oven, heat the vegetable oil over medium-high heat.
  2. Add the lamb or beef and cook until browned, about 5-7 minutes.
  3. Add the onions and cook until softened, about 3-5 minutes.
  4. Add the grated carrots and cook for another 2-3 minutes.
  5. Stir in the rice, making sure it is well coated with the oil and vegetables.
  6. Pour in the water or beef broth and season with salt and pepper.
  7. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat to low, cover the pot, and simmer for 20-25 minutes or until the rice is tender and the liquid is absorbed.
  8. Fluff the rice with a fork and serve hot.
